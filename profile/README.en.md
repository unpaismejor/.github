# Fundación Un País Mejor

## Technology for the Development of the Dominican Republic

**Fundación Un País Mejor** works to promote comprehensive human development in the Dominican Republic by supporting and advancing initiatives in the **social, economic, educational, and healthcare sectors**.

This GitHub organization brings together, develops, and supports **open-source projects** aligned with our mission. We aim to use technology as an accessible, sustainable, and collaborative tool to address real needs, strengthen institutions, and create opportunities that contribute to the country’s development.

## Our Mission

To promote initiatives and solutions that improve people’s quality of life, strengthen communities, and support the sustainable development of the Dominican Republic.

## Areas of Focus

Our projects may be related to:

* Social and community development.
* Education and access to knowledge.
* Health and well-being.
* Innovation and digital transformation.
* Economic development and institutional strengthening.
* Transparency, collaboration, and open access to technology.

## Open Source for a Better Country

We believe that open-source software makes it possible to share knowledge, reduce access barriers, and build solutions collectively.

For this reason, we promote projects that are:

* **Accessible:** designed for use by different individuals, communities, and institutions.
* **Collaborative:** open to contributions from developers, professionals, organizations, and communities.
* **Sustainable:** created with long-term maintenance, continuity, and growth in mind.
* **Transparent:** documented and developed through open processes.
* **Impact-driven:** focused on addressing specific needs within Dominican society.

## How to Contribute

Collaboration is an essential part of our vision. You can contribute in several ways:

1. Report bugs or suggest improvements.
2. Participate in the development of new features.
3. Improve project documentation.
4. Support project testing and validation.
5. Share knowledge, experience, and best practices.
6. Propose technology initiatives aligned with our mission.

Before contributing, please review the `CONTRIBUTING.md` file and the specific instructions provided in each repository.

## Community Principles

We expect all participants to help maintain a respectful, inclusive, and professional environment.

Interactions within our projects should be guided by:

* Respect.
* Collaboration.
* Transparency.
* Responsibility.
* Inclusion.
* Commitment to the common good.

Please review our `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md` file for the community participation guidelines.
